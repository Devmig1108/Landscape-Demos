Frequently Asked Questions

Expert insights for homeowners.

How do I know if my garage spring is broken?

A broken spring usually happens with a loud "bang." Afterwards, the door will be extremely heavy to lift manually, or your opener will strain and stop moving. Do not attempt to operate the door, as this can damage the motor. Call us immediately for a safe replacement.

Do you offer same-day garage repair in El Paso?

Yes! We prioritize emergency calls like broken springs, snapped cables, or off-track doors. We strive to provide same-day or next-day service to ensure your home remains secure.

Which garage door openers do you repair?

Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair all major brands, including LiftMaster, Chamberlain, and Genie. If your unit is beyond repair, we can install a modern, quiet, WiFi-enabled smart motor.
